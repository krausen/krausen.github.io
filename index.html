Simple rules for CI

Don’t branch (or at least keep them short lived) The goal of Continuous Integration is to integrate often. Long lived branches work against this goal and should be avoided. Strive towards having your changes in the remote master before the end of the day. Have a fast test suite You should not have to wait long for the tests to finish, a couple of minutes at the most. Fail fast, if a single test case fails it should be enough fail the entire test suite.
